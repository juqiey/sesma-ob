Our Legacy

Built on Grit,
Forged in the Scrum

Founded in 2024, our club was established to foster a culture of physical dominance and tactical intelligence. We don't just play rugby; we build leaders who understand the value of the man standing next to them.

"In the scrum, there is no individual—only the collective force of the pack."

From our humble beginnings on local dirt patches to our modern training facilities, the "SESMA Old Boys" philosophy remains the same: No retreat, no surrender.
